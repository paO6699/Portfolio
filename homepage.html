Hello, I'm Danish

As a Video Editor and Assistant Producer, I am passionate about transforming ideas into compelling visual stories. With a strong eye for detail and a creative approach, I enjoy bringing scripts to life, ensuring that every frame and sound is perfectly aligned to deliver an impactful message.

Work Process

1. Pre-Production (As an Assistant Producer)

Collaborate with the producer and creative team to brainstorm ideas the show's content.

2. Production (As an Assistant Producer)

Support the producer in managing the daily activities, ensuring that Production stays on schedule.

3. Post-Production (As an Assistant Producer and Video Editor)

Review all raw footage and organize it according to the script and production plan. Refine the edit by syncing visuals with voice-over, sound effects, and music. Apply color correction, transitions, and effects as needed.

4. Final Review & Delivery (As an Assistant Producer)

Conduct a final review of the edited program, ensuring there are no technical or content issues before broadcast. Deliver the polished final cut, ensuring it meets broadcast standards and complies with regulatory guidelines (e.g., KBBI and KPI).

5. Post-Broadcast (As an Assistant Producer)

Analyze the program’s performance using audience ratings (e.g., NIELSEN data) to assess how well the content resonated with viewers.
